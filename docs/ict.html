Boadi Yaw B. (HoD ICT)

The Department of Social Studies, popularly known as the General Arts Department, is one of the seven departments in Accra Wesley Girls’ SHS. Five subjects fall within the domain of the Department. They are Christian Religious Studies, Government, Economics, Social Studies and Geography. About seventeen tutors handle these subjects. Social Studies is one of the department’s subjects, which is being offered by the entire student body.

The department has recorded remarkable academic improvement on average, through commitment to duty by most of its members. A number of field trips and educational tours were organized by staff of the department. These educational tours played important roles in educating our learners in a practical and interesting style. The trips related to their academic work and they helped them learn much better. They also served as a way to have fun and recreation, which regularly rejuvenated them for improved performance.

Performance in WASSCE over the past four years has witnessed a steady increase on average in results relating to the department’s five subjects. In 2015, 36.2% of the students passed on average. It shot up to 52.5% passes in 2016. 62% was achieved in 2017 and it finally moved to 64.9% in the year 2018.
